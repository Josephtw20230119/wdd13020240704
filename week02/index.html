Taiwan

Have you heard about Taiwan? It’s this super awesome island in Asia that’s full of amazing things! Imagine tall buildings and bright lights in the capital city, Taipei, where you can try yummy bubble tea and tasty dumplings. But that’s not all! Taiwan has cool mountains to climb, beautiful beaches to play on, and even monkeys in the trees! The people are super nice and love to have fun festivals with fireworks and lanterns. Taiwan is like a giant treasure chest of fun and adventure. Why don't we visit right now?
